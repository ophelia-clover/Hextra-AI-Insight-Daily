2.  Protecting 3D point cloud content has always been a real head-scratcher when it comes to copyright, but a new paper just dropped a robust **deep neural watermarking framework** that's here to save the day! It tackles the problem by embedding binary watermarks into the singular values of point cloud blocks and then extracting them using a **PointNet++** neural network. The experiments are super promising, showing that even under heavy attacks like rotation, scaling, and cropping, this method's bit accuracy and Intersection over Union (IoU) still blow traditional methods out of the water, demonstrating stellar watermark recovery capabilities.
